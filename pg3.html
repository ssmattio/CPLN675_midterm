March 27, 2020: The Severity of the Pandemic is Realized

At this point, the severity of the situation was fully realized by most states. Most states initiated shelter-in-place orders and were desperate to acquire medical supplies, especially ventilators. The number of cases begins growing in other parts of the country, including Georgia and Ohio.
